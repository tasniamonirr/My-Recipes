An Elevated Grilled Cheese Sandwich

A grilled cheese sandwich is a tried-and-true meal when you're either a broke college student, someone who is too tired to make a 50 ingredient meal after a long day, or someone who simply yearns for the nostalgia. Whatever the case may be, you can never go wrong with a grilled cheesed sandwich. It only requires three ingredients can be made under 5 minutes. You'll never want to go back to a regular grilled cheese ever again after trying this recipe. It takes just as much time and effort, so why not take it to a whole new level?

Ingredients

  • 2 Sour Dough Bread slices
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp finely grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 slices of Gruyere cheese

Steps

  1. Place any sized skillet on stove on medium to low heat
  2. Melt butter on medium heat in the skillet for less than 1 minute
  3. Lower heat and add parmesan to the butter-coated skillet
  4. Add bread slices and let them toast until the parmesan forms a gliden-brown crust on each slice
  5. Once the crust is formed, while still on the skillet, place gruyere slices on on bread slice and place the second slice of bread on top to form a sandwich
  6. Turn heat down to lowest setting and place a cover on the skillet
  7. Once the cheese has melted, the sandwich is ready to eat!
