Elevated Mac & Cheese

Your favorite comfort food doesn't have to be boring. With a few extra ingredients, we can make the average macaroni and cheese a lot tastier in the same amount of time!

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice, you can choose your standard elbow macaroni or preferably spiral
  • 6 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 cup Grated parmesan and gruyere
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream
  • 1/8 cup Bread crumbs

Steps

  1. Put a skillet on low heat and melt a tbsp of butter to coat the pan
  2. Pour in bread crumbs and toast until gliden brown
  3. Follow instructions for your pasta to cook them until al-dente, cook time varies between brands
  4. Take the cooked pasta and drain it, but leave 1/2 cup of the pasta water for later
  5. Put 3 tbsp of butter on skillet on low heat along with the pasta, make sure all of the pasta is coated
  6. pour in half of the pasta water to allow the butter and pasta water to form a roux by stirring it frequently
  7. Evenly distribute cheese throughout pan and slowly pour in heavy cream and the rest of the butter as you continue mixing the pasta
  8. As you stir, you will notice the sauce begin to thicken and become more viscous as it simmers
  9. Once all of the ingredients have fully melted allow some time for pasta to cool
  10. When you are ready to eat, sprinkle the bread crumbs on top and enjoy!
